Delete your Miner Idle account

If you would like to permanently delete your Miner Idle account and all associated data, please send a request to:

support.flogames@gmail.com

Subject:

Delete Account Request

Please include your Google Play Games Player ID or the email associated with your account.

Data that will be deleted

  • Firebase Authentication account
  • Cloud Firestore save data
  • Game progress
  • Achievements linked to your account (where applicable)
  • Leaderboard related data stored by our services

Your request will usually be processed within 7 days.

Some information may be retained if required by applicable laws or for fraud prevention purposes.
