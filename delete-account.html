Delete Your Miner Idle Account

If you would like to delete your Miner Idle account and any associated personal data, you may request deletion by contacting the developer.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to YOUR_EMAIL_HERE.
  2. Use the subject: Delete Miner Idle Account.
  3. If possible, include your Player ID or the email address used to sign in.

What will be deleted

  • Your authentication account.
  • Your cloud save data.
  • Your game progress stored on our servers.
  • Associated account information stored in Firebase.

What may be retained

Some information may be retained if required by law or for fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or legal obligations.

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days after verification.
